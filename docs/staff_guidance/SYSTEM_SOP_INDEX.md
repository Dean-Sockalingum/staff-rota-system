## üìã Complete SOP Library

### 1. Staff Management

#### [SOP: Staff Onboarding](SOP_STAFF_ONBOARDING.md)
**Purpose:** Add new staff members to the system and set up their profiles  
**Users:** Administrators, HR Staff  
**Key Processes:**
- Quick onboarding (5-second setup)
- Bulk staff import
- Profile setup and permissions
- Initial training requirements

---

### 2. Scheduling & Rota Management

#### [SOP: Shift Management](SOP_SHIFT_MANAGEMENT.md)
**Purpose:** Create, view, and manage staff shifts and rotas  
**Users:** Unit Managers, Administrators, All Staff (viewing)  
**Key Processes:**
- Creating shift patterns
- Publishing weekly rotas
- Shift swaps and reallocations
- Handling shift conflicts
- Mobile rota viewing

---

### 3. Annual Leave

#### [SOP: Annual Leave](SOP_ANNUAL_LEAVE.md)
**Purpose:** Request, approve, and track annual leave  
**Users:** All Staff (requests), Unit Managers (approvals)  
**Key Processes:**
- Submitting leave requests
- Approving/declining requests
- Checking leave balances
- Managing carry-over and pro-rata calculations
- Leave usage targets and monitoring

---

### 4. Additional Staffing

#### [SOP: Additional Staffing](SOP_ADDITIONAL_STAFFING.md)
**Purpose:** Manage overtime shifts and agency staff bookings  
**Users:** Unit Managers, Senior Managers  
**Key Processes:**
- Creating overtime shifts
- Booking agency staff (8 approved agencies)
- Cost tracking and reporting
- Weekly staffing reports
- Agency rate management

---

### 5. Care Plan Reviews

#### [SOP: Care Plan Reviews](SOP_CARE_PLAN_REVIEWS.md)
**Purpose:** Complete and monitor care plan reviews for 120 residents  
**Users:** Keyworkers, Unit Managers  
**Key Processes:**
- Initial 4-week reviews
- 6-monthly ongoing reviews
- Completing review forms
- Manager approval workflow
- Compliance monitoring
- Overdue review management

---

### 6. Compliance & Reporting

#### [SOP: Compliance Reporting](SOP_COMPLIANCE_REPORTING.md)
**Purpose:** Generate compliance reports for Care Inspectorate and internal audits  
**Users:** Unit Managers, Senior Managers, Compliance Officers  
**Key Processes:**
- Care plan review compliance reports
- Training matrix reports
- Supervision records
- Incident reporting
- Annual leave compliance
- Additional staffing cost analysis

---

### 7. System Administration

#### [SOP: System Maintenance](SOP_SYSTEM_MAINTENANCE.md)
**Purpose:** Maintain system health, perform backups, and troubleshoot issues  
**Users:** System Administrators, IT Support  
**Key Processes:**
- Database backups
- Running management commands
- System updates
- Troubleshooting common issues
- User access management
- Email notification setup
