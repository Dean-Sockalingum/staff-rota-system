### Document 6: Reasonable Adjustments Guide

**What Are Reasonable Adjustments**:
- Changes to work/workplace to help disabled employees
- Legal requirement under Equality Act 2010

**Types of Adjustments**:
- Modified hours/flexible working
- Altered duties
- Equipment/assistive technology
- Additional breaks
- Home working
- Redeployment
- Phased return

**Implementing**:
- Discuss with employee
- Get OH advice
- Trial period
- Review effectiveness
- Document

### Document 7: Menopause in the Workplace

**Understanding Menopause**:
- Natural life stage (typically 45-55)
- Symptoms can significantly affect work
- Can last several years
- Protected characteristic (age/disability/sex)

**Common Symptoms Affecting Work**:
- Hot flushes
- Concentration/memory issues
- Fatigue
- Anxiety/low mood
- Sleep disturbance

**Reasonable Adjustments**:
- Temperature control (fans, near windows)
- Flexible working hours
- Additional breaks
- Uniform flexibility
- Access to cold water
- Private space if needed

**Having Conversations**:
- Confidential
- Supportive, not intrusive
- Focus on support not symptoms
- Signpost to resources

### Document 8: Occupational Health Referral (OHIO)

**When to Refer**:
- Need fitness for work advice
- Considering adjustments
- Prognosis unclear
- Return to work planning
- Disability assessment needed
- Multiple/recurring absences

**OHIO System**:
- Online referral portal
- Manager completes referral form
- OH triages and schedules
- Report sent to manager
- Discuss with employee

**OH Report Content**:
- Fitness for current role
- Prognosis and recovery timeline
- Recommended adjustments
- Equality Act considerations
- Return to work advice

**Acting on Recommendations**:
- Discuss report with employee
- Implement adjustments
- Review effectiveness
- Follow up if needed

### Document 9: Disability, Mental Health & Stress

**Equality Act 2010**:
- Definition of disability
- Protected characteristic
- Reasonable adjustments duty
- Discrimination types

**Managing Disability-Related Absence**:
- Separate from general sickness
- Don't penalize for disability absence
- Focus on adjustments
- Regular reviews

**Mental Health**:
- Common conditions (anxiety, depression, stress)
- Early intervention crucial
- EAP services
- OH referrals
- Stress risk assessments

**Stress Risk Assessment**:
- Required for stress-related absence
- Identify work-related stressors
- HSE Management Standards approach
- Implement controls
- Monitor effectiveness
