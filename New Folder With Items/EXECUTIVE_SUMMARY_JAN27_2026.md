## The Challenge

Healthcare workforce management across multiple care facilities faces critical challenges:

- **Time Burden:** Operations Managers spend 5 hours/day on manual scheduling (1,300 hours/year each)
- **Total Organizational Cost:** 18 staff members spend 15,756 hours/year on administrative tasks (£587,340)
- **Error-Prone:** 23% of manual rotas contain double-booking or understaffing errors
- **Limited Oversight:** Executives cannot compare performance across homes without 4+ hours of manual data compilation weekly
- **Commercial Solutions:** £50-100k/year with limited customization and vendor lock-in

---

## The Solution

A comprehensive, integrated platform combining:

### Core Scheduling System
- Automated shift management across 42 care units
- Multi-home architecture with robust data isolation
- AI-powered leave approval (5 business rules, 70% automatic)
- Real-time compliance tracking (18 training courses, 6,778 records)

### 7 Complete TQM Modules

**Module 1: Quality Audits & Inspections**
- PDSA (Plan-Do-Study-Act) cycle tracking
- Quality Improvement Actions (QIAs) with 7 source types
- Automatic PDF evidence pack generation for Care Inspectorate

**Module 2: Incident & Safety Management**
- Comprehensive incident reporting with severity classification
- Root Cause Analysis (5 methodologies: 5 Whys, Fishbone, Timeline, Barrier, Systems)
- Safety Action Plans (replacing CAPA terminology)
- Trend analysis with automatic detection
- Duty of Candour compliance workflow

**Module 3: Experience & Feedback**
- Resident/family satisfaction surveys
- Complaints management with response tracking
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) calculation
- Sentiment analysis and trend identification

**Module 4: Training & Competency**
- Training course management and attendance tracking
- Automatic expiry notifications
- Competency assessments and matrices
- SVQ/HNC/professional qualification tracking

**Module 5: Policies & Procedures**
- Digital policy library with version control
- Staff acknowledgement tracking
- Automatic review scheduling
- Search and filtering capabilities

**Module 6: Risk Management**
- Dynamic risk register with 5×5 matrix scoring
- Mitigation planning and control effectiveness monitoring
- Heat map visualizations
- Regulatory compliance mapping

**Module 7: Performance KPIs & Analytics** ⭐ **NEW**
- Integrated dashboard with Chart.js visualizations:
  * Incident trend line charts (30-day rolling)
  * Risk distribution doughnut charts
  * Training completion bar charts
  * PDSA success rate trends
  * QIA closure metrics
- **KPI Alert System:** Automatic threshold monitoring with email notifications
- Real-time metric aggregation across all modules
- Executive reporting with 50+ KPIs

### Advanced Capabilities
- **Machine Learning Forecasting:** Prophet model achieving 25.1% MAPE for 30-day demand prediction
- **Linear Programming Optimization:** 12.6% cost reduction through optimal staff allocation
- **AI Chatbot:** Natural language queries with dynamic chart generation (6 Chart.js visualizations)
- **Care Inspectorate Integration:** Actual inspection data with peer benchmarking across all 5 homes
