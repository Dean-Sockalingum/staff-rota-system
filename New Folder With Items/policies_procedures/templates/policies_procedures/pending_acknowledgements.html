About Policy Acknowledgements
Why do I need to acknowledge policies?

Policy acknowledgements ensure that all staff members have read, understood, and agree to comply with mandatory organizational policies. This is a regulatory requirement and helps maintain compliance standards.

What happens when I acknowledge a policy?
  • Your acknowledgement is recorded with a timestamp and digital signature
  • The system logs your IP address for audit purposes
  • You confirm that you have read and understood the policy
  • The policy is moved to your "Acknowledged" list
How often do I need to acknowledge policies?

You need to acknowledge policies when they are first published and whenever significant updates are made. The system will notify you when re-acknowledgement is required.
