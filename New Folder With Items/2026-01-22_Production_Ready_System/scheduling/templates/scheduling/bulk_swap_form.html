How Swapping Works

This operation will exchange ALL shifts between the two selected staff members:

  • All shifts assigned to Staff A will be reassigned to Staff B
  • All shifts assigned to Staff B will be reassigned to Staff A
  • Shift types, times, and units remain the same
  • Only shifts within the specified date range are affected

This operation cannot be easily undone
