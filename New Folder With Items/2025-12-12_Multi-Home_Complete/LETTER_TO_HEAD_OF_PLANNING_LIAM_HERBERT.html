Project Overview

The Intelligent Workforce Optimization Platform is a data-driven workforce management system built specifically for multi-site care providers. It combines traditional scheduling functionality with machine learning forecasting, predictive analytics, and automated optimization to transform reactive workforce management into strategic capacity planning.

The platform currently manages 5 care homes (550 beds, 42 units) with 1,354 staff and 133,658 automated shifts, processing real-time data to provide actionable insights for resource allocation and budget planning.
