## Purpose

This matrix ensures compliance with **Care Inspectorate** requirements for staff training and continuing professional development (CPD) in accordance with:

- Health and Social Care Standards (Scotland)
- Scottish Social Services Council (SSSC) Codes of Practice
- Care Inspectorate Quality Framework Theme 3 - Staffing

**Regulatory Requirements:**
- All care staff must complete mandatory training annually
- SSSC registered workers must complete minimum CPD hours
- Training must be evidenced with certificates
- Training matrix must be available during inspections
