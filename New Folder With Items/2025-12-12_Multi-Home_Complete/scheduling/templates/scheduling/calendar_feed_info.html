How to Subscribe

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Click the + button next to "Other calendars"
  3. Select "From URL"
  4. Copy and paste your HTTP URL (not webcal)
  5. Click "Add calendar"
  6. Your shifts will appear in Google Calendar and sync to your Android phone

Updates every few hours automatically

On iPhone/iPad:
  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll down to Calendar
  3. Tap Accounts → Add Account
  4. Tap Other → Add Subscribed Calendar
  5. Paste your Webcal URL
  6. Tap Next and Save
On Mac:
  1. Open Calendar app
  2. Go to File → New Calendar Subscription
  3. Paste your Webcal URL
  4. Click Subscribe
  5. Choose update frequency (recommended: Every hour)

Outlook.com (Web):
  1. Go to Outlook Calendar
  2. Click "Add calendar"
  3. Select "Subscribe from web"
  4. Paste your HTTP URL
  5. Enter a calendar name (e.g., "Work Shifts")
  6. Click "Import"
Outlook Desktop:
  1. Open Outlook
  2. Go to File → Account Settings → Internet Calendars
  3. Click "New"
  4. Paste your Webcal URL
  5. Click OK

Frequently Asked Questions
How often does the calendar update?

Your calendar app checks for updates periodically (usually every few hours). The exact frequency depends on your calendar app's settings.

Can I share this with my family?

Yes, but be careful - anyone with the URL can view your shifts. Only share with people you trust.

Will past shifts appear?

No, the feed only includes future shifts (next 8 weeks by default).

What if I change phones?

Just subscribe to the calendar on your new device using the same URL. Your shifts will sync automatically.

Can I unsubscribe?

Yes, simply remove the subscribed calendar from your calendar app.
