About the 5 Whys Method

The 5 Whys is an iterative interrogative technique used to explore cause-and-effect relationships. By repeatedly asking "Why?" (typically five times), you can peel away layers of symptoms to reveal the root cause of a problem. This method was developed by Sakichi Toyoda and was used within Toyota Motor Corporation during the evolution of its manufacturing methodologies.
