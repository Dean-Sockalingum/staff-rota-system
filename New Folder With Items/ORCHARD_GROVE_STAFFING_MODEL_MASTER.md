## 3-Week Rotation Pattern

### Pattern Structure

All staff follow a **repeating 3-week rotation cycle**:

- **Week 1**: Specific set of days (e.g., Sun, Mon, Tue)
- **Week 2**: Different set of days (e.g., Wed, Thu, Fri)
- **Week 3**: Third variation (e.g., Tue, Wed, Thu)
- **Then repeats** back to Week 1

### Example Patterns

#### 2 Shifts/Week (24 hours) - Typical Pattern

**Example Staff Member**: Daniel Cox (Orange Unit SCW)

```
Week 1: Sun, Mon (16 hours)
Week 2: Sun, Mon (16 hours)
Week 3: Sun, Mon (16 hours)
Total: 48 hours over 3 weeks = 16 hours/week average
```

#### 3 Shifts/Week (35-36 hours) - Typical Pattern

**Example Staff Member**: Leo Kelly (Orange Unit SCA)

```
Week 1: Sun, Mon, Tue (24 hours)
Week 2: Wed, Thu, Fri (24 hours)
Week 3: Tue, Wed, Thu (24 hours)
Total: 72 hours over 3 weeks = 24 hours/week average
```

#### Night Staff Example (SCWN)

**Jack Henderson (Pear Unit) - 3 shifts/week**

```
Week 1: Sun, Mon, Tue (3 √ó 12hr = 36 hours)
Week 2: Wed, Thu, Fri (3 √ó 12hr = 36 hours)
Week 3: Tue, Wed, Thu (3 √ó 12hr = 36 hours)
Total: 108 hours over 3 weeks = 36 hours/week average
```

### Day Distribution (0=Sunday through 6=Saturday)

Teams are designed to ensure **7-day-per-week coverage**:

- **Team A**: Often covers weekend + early week (Sun/Mon/Tue pattern)
- **Team B**: Often covers mid-week (Wed/Thu/Fri pattern)
- **Team C**: Often covers week transition (Tue/Wed/Thu or Thu/Fri/Sat pattern)

## Staffing Principles

### 1. Team Structure

Each unit operates with **Teams A, B, and C**:
- Ensures balanced coverage across all days
- Allows for predictable rotation patterns
- Facilitates peer support and continuity

### 2. Shift Distribution

**Day Shifts (08:00-20:00)**:
- Teams provide 24-27 shifts/week per unit
- Average 3.4-3.9 shifts per day per unit

**Night Shifts (20:00-08:00)**:
- Teams provide 20-31 shifts/week per unit
- Average 2.9-4.4 shifts per day per unit

### 3. Role Mix

**Each unit maintains**:
- **SCW (Social Care Workers)**: 30-40% of unit staff
- **SCA (Social Care Assistants)**: 50-60% of unit staff
- **SCWN (Night SCW)**: 10-15% of night staff
- **SCAN (Night SCA)**: 85-90% of night staff

### 4. Supernumerary Positions

**Not counted in staffing ratios**:
- Day SSCW Managers (1 per unit)
- Night SSCW Managers (1 per unit)
- Operations Managers (OM)
- Service Manager (SM)

### 5. Contractual Hours

**Staff work either**:
- **2 shifts/week**: ~16 hours/week (24-hour contract)
- **3 shifts/week**: ~24 hours/week (35-36 hour contract)
- **5 shifts/week**: ~40 hours/week (Management only)
