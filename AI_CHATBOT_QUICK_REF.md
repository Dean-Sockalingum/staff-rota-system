## Tips for Better Results

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Be specific with names: "Jane Smith" not "Jane"
- Include care home: "at Hawthorn House" or "at Orchard Grove"
- Add dates: "today", "this week", "December 25"
- Use proper roles: SCW, SCA, SSCW, OM, SM
- Ask one thing at a time

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Use abbreviations without context: "coverage" ‚Üí "coverage at [home]"
- Be too vague: "staff" ‚Üí "show me all staff at [home]"
- Mix multiple questions: Ask separately
- Use unclear pronouns: "he", "she", "it" ‚Üí use actual names
