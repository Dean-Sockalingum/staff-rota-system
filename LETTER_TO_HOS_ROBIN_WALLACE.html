Project Overview

The Intelligent Workforce Optimization Platform is a comprehensive staff scheduling and workforce management system designed to address the unique challenges faced by multi-home care providers. Built on Django and enhanced with artificial intelligence, the platform currently manages 5 care homes (550 beds across 42 units) with 1,354 staff members and 133,658 automated shifts.

What It Does

The system provides:

Core Capabilities (Currently Operational)

  • Multi-Home Architecture: Centralized management across all care homes with home-specific dashboards
  • Automated Leave Management: 73% of leave requests auto-approved within seconds using intelligent rule-based analysis
  • AI-Powered Assistant: Interactive chatbot that analyzes staffing shortages and provides actionable recommendations with one-click approval
  • Agency Coordination: Automated agency staff requests with 15-minute auto-approval timeouts
  • Compliance Monitoring: Real-time Working Time Directive (WTD) compliance with automatic violation prevention
  • Predictive Shift Generation: Uses Facebook Prophet ML to forecast 3-month shift patterns based on historical data

Enhanced Capabilities (Implementation Phase - 26 Weeks)

We are currently implementing 15 AI and automation enhancements that will transform the platform into a truly intelligent system:

Phase 1 - Foundation Layer (4 weeks)
1. Smart Staff Availability Matching: ML-powered system that ranks available staff for shortage coverage based on proximity, overtime load, skill match, preferences, and fatigue risk (96% reduction in response time: 15 minutes → 30 seconds)

  1. Multi-Agency Coordination: Auto-generates shift specifications and coordinates with 3 agencies simultaneously with real-time tracking (booking time: 2 hours → 10 minutes)

  2. Intelligent Shift Swap Auto-Approval: Replicates leave auto-approval success for shift swaps with 60% auto-approval rate

Phase 2 - Intelligence Layer (8 weeks)
4. Predictive Shortage Alerts: RandomForest ML model forecasts shortage risks 24 hours in advance with 75%+ accuracy using 10 features (sickness trends, historical patterns, leave conflicts)

  1. Real-Time Compliance Monitor: Auto-blocks WTD violations before they occur with live fatigue risk scoring

  2. AI-Powered Payroll Validator: Auto-calculates overtime, shift differentials, and bank holiday rates with anomaly detection (99% accuracy, 92% manual validation reduction)

  3. Budget-Aware Smart Recommendations: Cost-optimized staff allocation that prioritizes internal staff over agency (£450+ per shift savings)

Phase 3 - Advanced Intelligence (12 weeks)
8. NLP Incident Analyzer: Transformer-based classification of incident reports (falls, medication, behavioral) with severity scoring and auto-routing to appropriate teams

  1. Staff Wellbeing Monitor: Burnout risk detection using overtime trends, shift patterns, and absence data with proactive intervention alerts

  2. Automated Insights Generator: Weekly actionable recommendations dashboard with trend analysis and predictive alerts

  3. Leave Conflict Prediction: ML-powered detection of potential leave conflicts 2 weeks in advance

  4. Staff Onboarding Automation: Streamlined new hire workflow with auto-assignment of mentors, training schedules, and gradual shift integration
