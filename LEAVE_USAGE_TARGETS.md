## ğŸš¨ Common Issues & Solutions

### **Issue 1: "I'm too busy to take leave"**

**Response:**
- Taking leave is mandatory, not optional
- Arrange coverage in advance
- Break large periods into smaller chunks
- Book at least 1 day per fortnight

### **Issue 2: "I'm saving it for later in the year"**

**Risk:** Year-end rush, service disruption, lost leave

**Response:**
- Encourage even distribution
- Explain the 40-week strategy
- Set interim targets (quarterly)
- Make early leave booking mandatory

### **Issue 3: Team coverage concerns**

**Solutions:**
- Stagger leave across team
- Never allow more than X% off simultaneously
- Plan coverage 4 weeks in advance
- Use the rota system to visualize gaps

### **Issue 4: Excessive carryover requests**

**Prevention:**
- Monitor throughout year (not just December)
- Enforce quarterly targets
- Limit carryover to policy maximum
- Require justification for carryover
