## Regulatory Framework

This supervision session is conducted in accordance with:

- **Health and Social Care Standards (Scotland)** - Principle: "I have confidence in people because they are trained, competent and skilled"
- **SSSC Codes of Practice** - Workers must maintain clear and accurate records and participate in supervision
- **Care Inspectorate Quality Framework** - Theme 3: Staffing (How well is our staff team managed?)

**Supervision Frequency Requirements:**
- **Probationary staff:** Monthly minimum for first 6 months
- **Established staff:** Monthly or 6-weekly (service policy)
- **Senior staff:** Minimum 6-weekly
- **Contractors/agency:** As per contract (minimum 8-weekly)
