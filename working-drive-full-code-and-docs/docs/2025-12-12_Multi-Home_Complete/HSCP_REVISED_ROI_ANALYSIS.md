## Risk-Adjusted ROI

### Risk Factors & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact on Savings | Probability | Mitigation | Residual Risk |
|------|-------------------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| User adoption <80% | -15% savings | 20% | Mandatory training, champion network | 5% |
| Technical issues | -10% savings | 15% | 12-week phased rollout, pilot testing | 3% |
| Process change resistance | -20% savings | 25% | Change management program, manager buy-in | 8% |
| Overestimated baseline | -10% savings | 10% | External validation of current costs | 3% |

### Risk-Adjusted Calculation

```
Expected Savings = Base Savings × (1 - Weighted Risk)
Weighted Risk = (15% × 5%) + (10% × 3%) + (20% × 8%) + (10% × 3%)
Weighted Risk = 0.75% + 0.30% + 1.60% + 0.30% = 2.95%

Risk-Adjusted Savings = £651,181 × (1 - 0.0295)
Risk-Adjusted Savings = £631,967

Risk-Adjusted ROI = (£631,967 - £54,940) ÷ £54,940
Risk-Adjusted ROI = 1,051%
```

**Even with risk adjustment, ROI remains >1,050%**
