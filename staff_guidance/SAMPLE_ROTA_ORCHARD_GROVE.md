# Sample Rota - Orchard Grove Care Home

## 3-Week Rotation Pattern Guide

This document shows a sample 3-week rotation for Orchard Grove staff to help you understand your work pattern.

### Understanding Your Rota

- The rota operates on a **3-week cycle** that repeats continuously
- Each week is labeled as **Week 1, Week 2, or Week 3**
- After Week 3 completes, the cycle starts again at Week 1
- Your specific work days will repeat in the same pattern every 3 weeks

## Understanding Your Contract Hours

### 35-Hour Contracts
- Work **3 shifts per week** (3 x 12 = 36 hours, includes 1 hour paid break)
- Rotating days based on your assigned pattern
- Pattern changes each week in the 3-week cycle

### 24-Hour Contracts
- Work **2 shifts per week** (2 x 12 = 24 hours)
- Rotating days based on your assigned pattern
- Pattern changes each week in the 3-week cycle

## How to Read Your Personal Rota

1. **Find Your Name** - Check the online system or printed rota for your schedule
2. **Identify the Week** - Look for Week 1, Week 2, or Week 3 label
3. **Check Your Days** - Your work days are marked for that specific week
4. **Note the Pattern** - Your days will follow the same pattern when that week number repeats

### Example Reading:

If you see: **"Week 1: Sun, Mon, Tue"**
- You work Sunday, Monday, Tuesday in Week 1
- When Week 1 comes around again (3 weeks later), you'll work the same days
